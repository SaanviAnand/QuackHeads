About Us

PeriodHealth is an initiative by undergraduate students to spread awareness about menstruation. Such an issue becomes especially important in a country like India where menstruation is still an extremely taboo topic in most households. We aim to do this by providing job opportunities and distributing sanitary products.

SANITARY PRODUCTS

Here we have elaborated on some common menstrual products, their usage and benefits-

A variety of sanitary products are available in the market:
Pads –Made up of absorbent material which is stuck to the inside of the panties and absorbs menstrual blood.
Tampons –Temporarily inserted inside the vagina. Most tampons have a string for removal after use.
Menstrual cups –Made up of flexible medical-grade silicone. They are eco-friendly as they can be washed and reused. They are inserted inside the vagina to collect the menstrual blood.
